Walker Goddess Collective is a disability advocacy and consulting firm and the parent company of six branches spanning fashion, wellness, education, publishing, and technology. Built by a disabled founder, for everyone who deserves more.

Parent Company

Advocacy and Consulting

Because navigating systems should not require a law degree.

We provide disability advocacy, benefits navigation, accessibility consulting, and inclusive systems design for individuals and organizations. All services are delivered remotely.

Walker Goddess Collective is not a law firm and does not provide legal representation. Services offered are informational and advocacy-based in nature and do not constitute legal, social work, or clinical advice. We are not licensed attorneys or licensed social workers. While we are unable to provide legal services, we can offer general information relevant to your situation and connect you with appropriate legal and professional referrals.

Walker Goddess Collective was founded by a disabled woman whose lived experience, combined with ongoing education and community advocacy, forms the foundation of every service we provide. Our mission is to serve as a resource and voice for those who are navigating complex systems alone.
